ABOUT CYBERZ

Industry-specific cybersecurity resources that actually work.

Our Mission

"Generic security advice doesn't cut it. Every industry has unique threats, compliance requirements, and operational realities. We create resources that speak your language and solve your actual problems."

CyberZ was founded on a simple observation: cybersecurity resources are either too generic to be useful or too technical to be practical. A healthcare clinic and a real estate agency face completely different threats, yet most security guidance treats them the same.

We bridge that gap by creating tailored, actionable resources for specific industries — complete with policies, templates, checklists, and training materials that organizations can implement immediately.
